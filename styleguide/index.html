TV, Movie Tidbits Style Guide

Overview

Site Purpose

This site will contain random and/or interesting facts about tv shows and movies. These facts include but are not limited to: actors, music within the show or movie, director, and spinoffs in other media forms (e.g. graphic novels). Other content includes movie and tv posters and pictures and links to songs.

Target Audience

The age range for the target audience will be approximately 15-55 years old, and will appeal to both genders but geared a little more towards women. Residents of the USA and Canada are the main target group. Those who visit this site will mostly live in urban areas and will have an average income of $20,000. They access the internet primarily by their phone but use a laptop often as well.

Color Palette

I chose this color palette for a couple of reasons. I designed the logo before chosing any colors so I could incorporate the colors used in the logo in my site. All the colors are pulled from the TV in the logo with a shade or tint change as needed to create a pleasing, light feel to the page. I also chose these colors because three of them are primary colors and I want this site to be the primary source of media facts for everyone.

Typography

I chose Nanum Pen Script as the font for the headings because it invokes the feelings of writing out your own movie posters when you were a kid. It gives the site a more personal feel to it. I chose Varela Round for the body copy because it matches the tone of of the heading font. i like that the tips of the letters are rounded to work cohesively with the Nanum Pen Script font.
